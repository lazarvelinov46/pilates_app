This Privacy Policy describes how Pilates Studio Lilla ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01 Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register or use the App:

  • Account information — your name, surname, and email address, provided during registration.
  • Authentication data — your email and encrypted password, managed securely by Firebase Authentication.
  • Booking records — session bookings you make, including session date, time, and booking status.
  • Promotion data — details of session packages you have purchased, including usage history.
  • Session ratings — optional star ratings and comments you submit after attending sessions.
  • Notification preferences — your choice to enable or disable push notifications within the App.
  • Device token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used solely to deliver push notifications to your device.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is listed above.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the App's functionality:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process and display your session bookings and cancellations.
  • To manage your session promotion credits accurately.
  • To send push notifications about booking confirmations, session reminders, and studio cancellations — only if you have enabled them.
  • To allow studio administrators to manage sessions and view attendance.
  • To improve the quality of our classes through aggregated, anonymous ratings data.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

03 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database provided by Google LLC, with servers located in the United States and the European Union. Firebase is compliant with GDPR and other major data protection frameworks.

Access to your data is protected by Firebase security rules. Only you can read and modify your own account data. Studio administrators can view booking and attendance data necessary for operating the studio, but cannot access your password or payment information.

Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication using industry-standard encryption. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, though no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

04 Push Notifications

The App may send push notifications to your device using Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM). Notifications are used for:

  • Booking confirmation messages.
  • Reminders before an upcoming session.
  • Studio-initiated session cancellation notices with credit refund confirmation.

You can disable push notifications at any time through the App's Preferences screen, or through your device's system notification settings. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to use any other feature of the App.

05 Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Messaging, Cloud Functions) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — used optionally for account authentication — policies.google.com/privacy

We do not integrate with any advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers.

06 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law. Anonymised or aggregated data (such as general session attendance counts) may be retained indefinitely.

07 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and associated data.
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection — object to specific uses of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

08 Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us and we will promptly delete that information.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will notify you through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
